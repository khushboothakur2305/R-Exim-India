Mini-Kachori

Mini-Kachori is a deep fried crispy and crunchy ball of maida flour stuffed with moong dal and various spicy dry masala. it a mild sweet and spicy dry masala wrapped in a crisp outer layer of maida, is delicious snack that can be stored for few weeks.Unlike normal Kachories, the product remains fresh and preserved upto few weeks to couple of months.
It can be served at tea-parties, packed at picnics to munch when hunger strikes and also a great gifting option for those who prefer savoury over sweets.
You can make various kachori chat by topping as roasted cumin seeds, black pepper, red chili powder, sev bhujiya, pomegranate seeds, curd, green chutney, sweet chutney,coriander leaves on it.

RH-10 Rice

RH-10 is a hybrid strain of non-basmati rice. It has been developed by the Indian Agricultural Research Institute (IARI). This type of rice has the capacity to yield upto seven tonnes of paddy per hectare and matures 10-15 days earlier than the traditional basmati rice. Available Rice Forms
• Sella/Parboiled Rice
• Golden Sella/Parboiled Rice
• Steam Rice

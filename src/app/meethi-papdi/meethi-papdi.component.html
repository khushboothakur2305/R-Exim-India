Methi-Papdi

A blast of flavor and a attack of crunchiness....
Your taste buds are in for an exciting adventure with these methi papdi!A traditional Indian jar snack , these crisp Methi Puris are made with a dough of whole wheat flour, spruced up with fenugreek leaves and spices .
The mild bitterness and strong aroma of methis are really enchanting, and sure to tantalize your tast buds . you can serve the methi puris with tea to make it a special evening!

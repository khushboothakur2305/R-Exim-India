1121 Basmati Rice

1121 Basmati Rice that we are offering has high quality and the length of rice is 8.00 to 8.30 mm. It is longest basmati grain in the world before and after cooking. The elongation percentage of the grain is more than any other variety of the Basmati Rice. It gives 30% more volume than other basmati variety. This variant can be recognized by its unique aroma and subtle sweet taste.
Varieties of Basmati Rice Available:-
• 1121 RAW
• 1121 Sella
• 1121 steam

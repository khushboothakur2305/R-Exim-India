Puffed-Poha

One of the most popular breakfast recipes throughout India, poha has a variety of recipes.
We bring to you one such unique version of poha, Puffed Rice Poha. Generally poha is prepared using flat rice but here we curate a recipe of poha with puffed rice or as commonly known, murmura.
Prepared with easily available ingredients like onion, potato, puffed rice, and curry leaves, this scrumptious snack would definitely cater to your taste buds. You can pack this recipe in your lunch boxes to savor its taste as a quick in between work bite or even carry it as a en route breakfast whenever running late for work.
